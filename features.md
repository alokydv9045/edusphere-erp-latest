# EduSphere: The Future of School Management
**Comprehensive Feature Catalog & Ecosystem Overview**

EduSphere is a next-generation School ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) system designed to maximize administrative efficiency, academic excellence, and student engagement through cutting-edge technology and AI integration.

---

## 🧠 AI & Intelligent Automation
*The "Academic Oracle" Suite*

### ✨ AI Academic Oracle
> *"Your Schools Neural Engine"*
- **Personalized Assistant**: A context-aware AI for Students and Teachers that answers questions about grades, schedules, and school policies.
- **Role-Based Intelligence**: Tailored interfaces for students (learning help) and teachers (administrative help).

### 🖋️ Smart Assignment Assistant
> *"Drafting Excellence in Seconds"*
- **Source-Driven Generation**: Generate high-quality assignments based on custom reference notes.
- **Multi-Format Questions**: Instant creation of MCQs, One-Word, and Descriptive tasks.
- **Auto-PDF Engine**: Automatically generates professional reference material PDFs for student downloads.

*Precision in Learning Management*

### 🏫 Academic Structure Manager
- Granular control over Classes, Sections, and Subjects.
- **Smart Timetabling**: Conflict-free scheduling for complex school rotations.

### 📝 Assignment Hub
- **Digital Library**: Centralized repository for all classwork.

### 📊 Exam & Result Management

## 🕒 Attendance Management
*Seamless Presence Tracking*

### 📲 Hybrid Attendance System
> *"Zero-Effort Verification"*
- **Manual Marking Interface**: Rapid classroom attendance entry for teachers with multi-status support (Present, Absent, Late, Half-day).
- **QR-Based Automation**: Dynamic QR scanning via the mobile app for instant student/staff entry logs.
- **RFID Integration**: Support for hardware scanners at entry gates for hands-free automation and instant SMS alerts to parents.
- **Deep Analytics**: Real-time attendance percentage tracking for individual students and entire classes.

## 🚀 Technology Stack
*   **Frontend**: Next.js 14, Tailwind CSS, Lucide Icons, Shadcn UI.
*   **Backend**: Node.js, Express, Prisma ORM.
*   **Database**: PostgreSQL (Supabase / Neon).
*   **AI Engine**: Google Gemini 3 (Flash Preview).
*   **Security**: JWT Authentication with Role-Based Access Control (RBAC).
